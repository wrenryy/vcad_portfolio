A LITTLE 'BOUT ME
 

I've always been drawn to the overlap between concept and design. Utilising Adobe software (like InDesign and Illustrator) to develop out-of-this-world templates, logos and more, and bringing them to life through HTML, CSS and Javascript.

From creating Howrse templates in my early adolescence to attempting to hack my own computer, my passion of computer programming has has blossomed it's roots. I got an education from Robertson College (2018-2019) and improved my abilities in the industry. Since then I've mainly freelanced my skills wherever they are needed.

What once started as nothing but a hobby in my early teens with the infuence of freelancing: both concept art and graphic design have become a full-time passion and I've persued into more education at Visual College of Art and Design (VCAD, 2021-2022).
